Our Climbing Locations

Mount Kenya

Africa's second highest peak (5,199m) with stunning glacial valleys and diverse wildlife. Perfect for technical climbs and scenic hikes.

Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest free-standing mountain (5,895m). Our most popular climb with multiple route options for all skill levels.

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Ngong Hills

Beautiful ridge (2,460m) with panoramic views of Nairobi. Ideal for beginners and day hikes.

Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆

Mount Elgon

Ancient volcano (4,321m) with the largest surface area in Africa. Features spectacular caves and waterfalls.

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

Alpine Mountains

Europe's most famous mountain range. We offer guided climbs in the Swiss, French, and Italian Alps.

Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Mount Everest

The world's highest peak (8,848m). Our premium expedition with full support for experienced climbers.

Difficulty: ★★★★★
